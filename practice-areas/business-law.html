Business Law

Most large corporations have an “in-house counsel,” a lawyer that the executives regularly turn to for advice and legal expertise. Regardless of whether you run a small, medium or large business, retaining a costly in-house counsel may not be in your budget. Retaining Marsicano + Leyva PLLC as your business attorneys or corporate lawyers is the next best thing as they can serve as your affordable general counsel.


Marsicano + Leyva PLLC is dedicated to providing its business, franchise, and startup entrepreneurial clients with valuable legal guidance and representation on issues prevalent throughout the life of the company, from formation to wind-down. Whether it’s a routine contract or complex transaction, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC will work with you to increase your company’s overall performance and bottom line. Aside from a traditional hourly fee structure, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC offers a variety of unbundled legal services at a flat rate.


Our law firm represents investors, shareholders, corporations, and business entities throughout the state. The business law areas that Marsicano + Leyva PLLC concentrates in include:

  • - Startup Law/Business formation:
    We are intimately familiar with the business entity models available under the law, including corporations, limited liability companies (LLCs) and limited partnerships. We handle entity registration, articles of incorporation, bylaws, minutes, operating agreements and all other aspects of business formation.
  • - Professional associations:
    Organizations of professionals — such as architects, accountants, attorneys and physicians — may be subject to certain licensing restrictions that impact business entity formation. We are experienced at addressing these issues.
  • - Commercial Contracts:
    We are experienced at drafting and negotiating sophisticated commercial contracts on behalf of business clients.
  • - Employment contracts:
    Florida and federal law place constraints on the provisions that may be included in employment contracts. We draft and review contracts and employee manuals to meet employers' needs while ensuring compliance with the law.
  • - Business dissolution:
    When the owners of a business no longer wish to do business together, we handle the dissolution of the entity and distribution of assets that requires careful attention from lawyers who are prepared to handle any disputes that may arise.

Starting a new business is an exciting and often confusing endeavor. At Marsicano + Leyva PLLC, we understand that selecting the right corporate form is crucial to protecting your assets and minimizing liability. Our Florida business lawyers counsel clients on the entire process and everything involved, including the seemingly never-ending paperwork associated with launching a new business.


We can work with you on a wide range of matters, including:

  • - Finding financing
  • - Cretaing a business plan
  • - Navigating paperwork
  • - Formation
  • - Licensing
  • - State Agency Filings

In addition to guiding your startup through all aspects of formation, Marsicano + Leyva PLLC can help make sure that your jurisdictional questions are well-settled, allowing you to conquer the global market with confidence. We take the guesswork out of startup so you are free to focus on what's most important - profit.
