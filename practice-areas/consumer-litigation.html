Consumer Litigation

Our attorneys are passionate about defending you from predatory and aggressive behavior stemming from unethical debt collection practices. If you are being harassed regarding your debt, you may be entitled to compensation under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act, the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act, the Florida Consumer Collection Practices Act, the Fair Credit Reporting Act, and the Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act. Whether its foreclosure or credit card defense, our attorneys have experience on both sides of the issues enabling them to provide you with valuable and realistic insight into your case.


We put a stop to harassing phone calls from telemarketers and robocalls, making full use of the Telephone Consumer Protection Act to enforce violations with penalties of up to $1,500 per violation. We also put an end to embarrassing, threatening, and intimidating debt collection practices made unlawful by the Federal Debt Collection Practices Act and the Florida Consumer Collections Act. We can additionally help you fix errors on your credit report and collect money damages for harmful credit errors under the Fair Credit Reporting Act. Other major areas of our consumer law practice include identity theft, deceptive trade practices, lemon law and auto fraud, and student loan law.


Consumers have significant rights and are often taken advantage of by unscrupulous providers, creditors, and lenders. We understand consumer rights and how they apply to your specific situation. We will review your case and diligently prepare your defense to ensure that your rights are protected.


Understand Your Rights

You have rights as a consumer. We are committed to protecting your rights and guiding your defense. We can help if you have been sued by a credit card company, debt collector, lender, bank, or other creditor.
